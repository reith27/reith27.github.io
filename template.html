Stickrunner

My first programming project in City is Stickrunner. It is a game where you have to reach the portal with at least one life remaining. You have to try and avoid the dragons as the green dragons remove one life and the red dragons remove two lives. You can use as many crates as you'd like to make the run easier. Both lives left and crates used can be seen on the top left.
